Batam As Hub

Indonesia’s data center market is booming, projected to grow from $1.62 billion in 2023 to $3.40 billion by 2032. This surge is driven by the rapid adoption of cloud computing, increased digital transformation across industries, and the rising use of data-intensive applications.

Batam's strategic location further amplifies its appeal as a data center hub. The island hosts multiple cable landing stations. This enhance connectivity between Indonesia, Singapore, and the broader region. By choosing Batam for your data hosting needs, you leverage a location that offers robust infrastructure, strategic connectivity, and compliance with local regulations, positioning your business for success in Indonesia's burgeoning digital landscape.

Elevating Infrastructure, Empowering Growth

Positioned at the heart of Southeast Asia’s digital corridor, Batam is a powerhouse of connectivity and resilience. With 12 operational subsea cables linking Indonesia, Singapore, Malaysia, and Thailand and 3 more under construction. Batam offers an unparalleled advantage for businesses seeking low-latency, high-speed digital infrastructure. Strategically located just 7 km from 6 major cable landing stations, our data center ensures direct access to robust interconnectivity, making it an ideal hub for regional and international operations.

Beyond connectivity, power security is guaranteed through a stable supply from PLN Batam, while ample water resources from the industrial estate ensure sustainable operations. Designed for maximum reliability, our data center is safely positioned outside Indonesia's ring of fire and the landing envelope, strategically positioned to avoid uncontrolled disruptions. Accessibility is also a key advantage our facility is seamlessly connected to the city via 8-meter-wide roads, ensuring smooth logistics and operational efficiency.

WHY BATAM?

Tax Incentives
Batam offers attractive tax  benefits, including import duty exemptions, VAT incentives, and special economic zone (SEZ) advantages. These incentives significantly reduce investment costs, making it a cost-effective location for data centers and digital businesses.
Strategic Landscape
Batam is strategically positioned SIJORI (Singapore - Johor – Riau Islands) triangle, with direct subsea cable connectivity to Indonesia, Malaysia, and Thailand. Its industrial zoning allows for scalable infrastructure, ensuring smooth operations without urban constraints. Plus, it’s just a short ferry ride from Singapore, making accessibility effortless.
Future-Proof for Green Growth
With access to renewable energy sources, abundant water supply, and strict environmental regulations, Batam is embracing sustainable development. Businesses can reduce their carbon footprint while benefiting from a greener, more responsible ecosystem aligning with global ESG commitments.
